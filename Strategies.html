Offensive Strategies

Offensive Tips:

  • Clean breaks
  • Cover angles
  • Be agressive
  • Communicate
  • Tag enemies
  • Plan routes

Offensive Positions

Forward Center and Right/Left (Depending on Snake):
- Both players must make a clean break and position themselves so they can cut off the sharp angle into the snake or opposite side.
- Once position is stable and opposing players' positions are known, slowly move your shot to the next bunker, moving towards the back center.


Defensive Strategies

Defensive Tips:

  • Constant shot
  • Hug Bunker
  • Stay Planted
  • Communicate
  • Cut angles

Defensive Positions

Back Right, Center, and Left:
- Two of the three must cover the side opposite to the snake o the pressure will be lifted from that direction.
- Stick to a lane that allows you to control the movements across the field from the opposing team.


Snake Strategies

Snake Tips:

  • Stay low
  • Say quiet
  • Hug bunker
  • Hold tight
  • Cut angles

Snake Position

Snake:
- Stay low and crawl into planned position and attempt to cut off people across from the snake
one your back can cover your other side
- Make sure you are aware of other activity in the snake from the other team
- Do not over-push your position since the snake holds the opposing team from moving forward.
